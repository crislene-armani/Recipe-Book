Mousse de Maracujá - Passion Fruit Mousse

Back to Homepage

Mousse de Maracujá is a creamy and tangy Brazilian dessert made with passion fruit juice, sweetened condensed milk, and cream. This quick and easy recipe is a favorite for its refreshing flavor and simplicity, making it a perfect choice for any occasion.

Watch the Preparation Video

Ingredients

  • 1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can (14 ounces) heavy cream (or crème fraîche)
  • 1 cup passion fruit juice (fresh or concentrated)
  • Optional: Passion fruit pulp with seeds for topping

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine the sweetened condensed milk, heavy cream, and passion fruit juice. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  2. Pour the mixture into serving glasses or a large serving dish.
  3. Refrigerate for at least 2-3 hours, or until the mousse is set and chilled.
  4. If desired, spoon a small amount of passion fruit pulp with seeds on top of each serving for garnish.
  5. Serve cold and enjoy this delightful dessert!
