Lentil Soup

Back to Homepage

Lentil Soup is a hearty and nutritious dish made with tender lentils, vegetables, and savory seasonings. It's perfect for cozy dinners and can be enjoyed as a filling, plant-based meal or served alongside your favorite protein.

Watch the Preparation Video

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 medium carrots, peeled and diced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 cup dried lentils, rinsed
  • 6 cups vegetable broth or water
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric (optional)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice (optional, for added brightness)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the diced carrots and chopped celery to the pot. Cook for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Stir in the rinsed lentils, cumin, turmeric (if using), and bay leaf.
  4. Pour in the vegetable broth (or water) and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat and let the soup simmer for 25â€“30 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
  5. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste. If desired, add lemon juice for added brightness.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with fresh chopped parsley. Lentil soup is delicious with a slice of crusty bread or a side salad.
