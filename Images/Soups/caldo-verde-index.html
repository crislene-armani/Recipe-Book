Caldo Verde / Green Soup

Back to Homepage

Caldo Verde is a soup made with potatoes, kale, chorizo, and garlic. This hearty and comforting soup is often served during celebrations and family meals.

Watch the Preparation Video

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 bunch kale, stemmed and thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 pound chorizo sausage, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: 1 bay leaf for extra flavor

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, cooking until softened and fragrant, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the diced potatoes, paprika, and chicken broth to the pot. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer until the potatoes are tender, about 15 minutes.
  3. While the soup is simmering, in a separate pan, fry the chorizo slices until crispy, about 5–7 minutes. Set aside.
  4. Once the potatoes are cooked, use a potato masher or immersion blender to mash or blend the soup until smooth, leaving some chunks for texture.
  5. Add the sliced kale to the soup and simmer for another 5–7 minutes until the kale is tender.
  6. Stir in the cooked chorizo and season with salt and pepper to taste. If desired, add a bay leaf for extra flavor during the simmering process, then remove before serving.
  7. Serve hot with a drizzle of olive oil and enjoy your Caldo Verde with crusty bread on the side!
