Canja de Galinha / Chicken Soup

Back to Homepage

Canja de Galinha is a comforting Brazilian chicken soup made with tender chicken, rice, and vegetables. It is often enjoyed as a light meal or a remedy when you're feeling under the weather. This traditional dish is simple, hearty, and perfect for any occasion.

Watch the Preparation Video

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 chicken thighs or breasts (with bone for added flavor)
  • 6 cups chicken broth or water
  • 1/2 cup rice (preferably short-grain)
  • 2 medium carrots, peeled and diced
  • 1/2 cup peas (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and cook until softened and fragrant, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the chicken thighs (or breasts) to the pot and brown them on all sides, about 5–7 minutes.
  3. Pour in the chicken broth or water, bring the mixture to a boil, then lower the heat and let it simmer for about 20–25 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked and tender.
  4. Remove the chicken from the pot and shred it using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the pot.
  5. Add the rice, diced carrots, and peas (if using). Simmer the soup for another 15 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the vegetables are cooked through.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. If desired, add more chicken broth or water to adjust the consistency of the soup.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with fresh chopped parsley. Enjoy your Canja de Galinha with a slice of bread or crackers on the side!
