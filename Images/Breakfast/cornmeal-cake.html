Bolo de Fubá (Cornmeal Cake)

Back to Homepage

This traditional Brazilian cornmeal cake is light, fluffy, and perfect with coffee or tea. It's a staple in Brazilian households and incredibly easy to make.

Watch the Preparation Video

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix the cornmeal, flour, and sugar in a bowl.
  3. Add melted butter, milk, and eggs; stir until smooth.
  4. Fold in the baking powder.
  5. Pour into a greased baking pan and bake for 30–35 minutes.
