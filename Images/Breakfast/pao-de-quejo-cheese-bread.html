Pão de Queijo (Cheese Bread)

Back to Homepage

Pão de Queijo, or Brazilian cheese bread, is a chewy, cheesy, gluten-free treat. These delightful bites are made with tapioca flour and cheese, perfect as a snack or breakfast item.

Watch the Preparation Video

Ingredients

  • 2 cups tapioca flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 large eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease a muffin tin or line with parchment paper.
  2. In a saucepan, combine milk, butter, and salt, and bring to a boil.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in the tapioca flour until smooth.
  4. Let the mixture cool for a few minutes, then mix in the eggs one at a time.
  5. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until fully incorporated.
  6. Using a spoon or piping bag, fill the muffin tin with the batter.
  7. Bake for 20–25 minutes, or until the cheese bread is puffed and golden brown.
