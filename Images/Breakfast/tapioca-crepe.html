Tapioca Crepe

Back to Homepage

Tapioca crepes, known as *tapiocas* in Brazil, are a gluten-free delight made from cassava starch. They're quick, versatile, and can be filled with savory or sweet ingredients to suit your taste.

Watch the Preparation Video

Ingredients

  • 1 cup tapioca starch (hydrated)
  • A pinch of salt
  • Optional: butter or oil for cooking
  • Filling of your choice (e.g., cheese, ham, Nutella, fruits)

Instructions

  1. Sift the tapioca starch into a bowl to remove lumps.
  2. Mix in a pinch of salt for flavor.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  4. Spread a thin layer of the tapioca starch evenly over the skillet, covering the bottom.
  5. Cook for about 1â€“2 minutes, or until the crepe holds together and the edges lift slightly.
  6. Flip the crepe and cook the other side for another minute.
  7. Add your desired filling to one half of the crepe, fold it over, and serve immediately.
