Vinagrete (Brazilian Vinaigrette)

Back to Homepage

Vinagrete is a Brazilian vinaigrette that is typically served as a side dish or condiment for barbecues and grilled meats. Made with fresh vegetables and a tangy dressing, it is a refreshing and zesty accompaniment for any meal.

Watch the Preparation Video

Ingredients

  • 2 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro or parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar (optional, to balance acidity)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, chopped onion, cucumber, and cilantro (or parsley).
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, mustard (if using), salt, pepper, and sugar (if desired).
  3. Pour the dressing over the vegetable mixture and toss well to combine.

  5. Serve as a side dish or topping for grilled meats, rice, or other dishes. Best served chilled or at room temperature.
