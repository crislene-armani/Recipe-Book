Salpic達o / Chicken Salad

Back to Homepage

Salpic達o is a traditional Brazilian salad, often served during festive occasions. It combines shredded chicken, vegetables, and a creamy mayonnaise dressing, creating a refreshing and hearty dish perfect for barbecues or family gatherings.

Watch the Preparation Video

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked and shredded chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup carrots, grated or thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas, cooked
  • 1/2 apple, diced
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels (fresh or canned)
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream or sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup green olives, sliced (optional)
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley or cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the chicken breast until fully cooked, then shred it into small pieces using two forks or your hands.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the shredded chicken, grated carrots, peas, diced apple, and corn kernels.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, heavy cream (or sour cream), mustard, salt, and pepper to create the dressing.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad mixture and toss gently to combine, ensuring everything is well-coated.
  5. If desired, add green olives for an extra flavor boost, and mix them into the salad.
  6. Chill the salad in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving to allow the flavors to meld.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley or cilantro before serving. Serve cold.
