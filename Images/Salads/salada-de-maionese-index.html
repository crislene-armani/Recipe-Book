Salada de Maionese Cremosa / Creamy Mayonnaise Salad

Back to Homepage

Salada de Maionese Cremosa is a beloved Brazilian salad made with a creamy mayonnaise dressing, potatoes, and vegetables. It's a perfect side dish for barbecues, picnics, or any family gathering.

Watch the Preparation Video

Ingredients

  • 4 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 carrot, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup green peas, cooked
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup sour cream or heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Boil the diced potatoes and carrots in salted water until tender, about 10â€“12 minutes. Drain and let cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked potatoes, carrots, and green peas.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, sour cream (or heavy cream), mustard, salt, and pepper to create the dressing.
  4. Pour the dressing over the vegetables and gently mix to combine.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving to let the flavors meld.
  6. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve cold.
